# The Standard Team — Core — Rules

## Purpose Definition

**tst-core-001** [ERROR] Every team and project must document its business nature — what the business is.
**tst-core-002** [ERROR] Every team and project must document its business goals — the desired outcomes the business pursues.
**tst-core-003** [ERROR] Every team and project must document its future considerations — growth plans and expansion scenarios.
**tst-core-004** [ERROR] All three purpose elements (nature, goals, future considerations) must be present before engineering work begins.

## Scenarios

**tst-core-005** [ERROR] Scenarios must include an actor (Given), an interaction (When), and an outcome (Then).
**tst-core-006** [ERROR] Scenarios must be written in non-technical language unless the business itself is technical in nature.
**tst-core-007** [ERROR] Scenario outcomes must be expressible as marketing copy understandable by non-technical stakeholders.
**tst-core-008** [WARN]  Scenarios may chain — each subsequent scenario may build on the established context of prior scenarios.
**tst-core-009** [ERROR] Scenarios must represent the perspective of end users or customers — not developers or system components.
**tst-core-010** [WARN]  Multiple scenarios should be written to cover all primary actors and major use cases of the system.

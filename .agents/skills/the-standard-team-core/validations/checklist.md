# The Standard Team — Core — Checklist

- [ ] Business nature is documented — describes what the business is (tst-core-001)
- [ ] Business goals are documented — describes desired outcomes (tst-core-002)
- [ ] Future considerations are documented — describes growth or expansion plans (tst-core-003)
- [ ] All three purpose elements are present before engineering work proceeds (tst-core-004)
- [ ] Each scenario includes an actor (GIVEN), interaction (WHEN), and outcome (THEN) (tst-core-005)
- [ ] Scenarios are written in non-technical language (tst-core-006)
- [ ] Scenario outcomes are understandable by non-technical stakeholders (tst-core-007)
- [ ] Scenarios represent end-user or customer perspective — not developer perspective (tst-core-009)
